Webinar on Machine Learning Engineering by Carlos Mougan

The chapter organized a webinar on the topic "Machine Learning Engineering" which focussed on how to structure a real world ML Project and the various stages involved for getting it into production through a case study. The webinar was conducted by Carlos Mougan who is a Data Scientist at European Central Bank in Frankfurt and was the facilitator for our project at the Turing Institute. The session was attended by over 60 students from SNU & 20 additional attendees from European Central Bank, Frankfurt.

Two day Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence Workshop

We organized a workshop for freshmen and sophomores from SNU as well as nearby colleges in collaboration with Cognizance, IIT Roorkee. This program consisted of 2-days workshop on the topic " Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence". The workshop was delivered by a top-class professional cum industry expert, Dr. Manoj Chaudhary and consisted of both theoretical and practical sessions. All the participants were provided with two certificates of participation from Cognizance IIT Roorkee and ACM, SNU.

Actions on Google(Assistant) Workshop

A virtual workshop was held by the Chapter in association with Mr. Vijay Nandwani, CEO of Espresso Labs and an alumnus of Shiv Nadar University. In this workshop, the attendees were introduced to the Firebase platform and were instructed on how to develop interactive voice and graphical applications. The pay-off was creating an interactive canvas game that ran from inside Google Assistant.
